    • About Me



    My name is Emily Bresnahan and I am a 22 year old senior who attends Babson College. For the past two years I have played on the Division III Women's Lacrosse team and this year we won the NEWMAC Championship. Before transfering to Babson College I played Division I Women's Lacrosse at the University of Colorado. Lacrosse is a sport I grew up playing and in turn has become apart of my identity. Thank you for visiting my website and I hope it shows you how meaningful a sport can be to someone!
