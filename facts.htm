Fun Facts About Lacrosse

Fact 1:

Lacrosse is one of the fastest growing sports in North America, Europe, and Australia.

Fact 2:

Native Americans originally invented the game but details about how it was played still remain a mystery today.

Fact 3:

Lacrosse is played at 600 colleges and 2,00 high schools in America.

Fact 4:

Lacrosse is the fastest sport to be played on two feet.
